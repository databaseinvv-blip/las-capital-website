About LAS Capital

Discipline at every step

An investment fund built to deliver capital gain to Unit Holders

LAS Capital is a private equity fund established to invest in companies and assets across any sector — through merger & acquisition, share investment, VC/PE funding and debt financing.

The Fund pursues a balance of capital appreciation and stable returns: value investing in brand leaders with growth prospects, growth investing in businesses compounding net profit at double digits, and profit-sharing financing across supply chain, equipment, plantation and machinery arrangements.

Offered exclusively to Sophisticated Investors as defined under Part 1, Schedule 7 of the Capital Markets and Services Act 2007. The Investment Manager, Aureus Strategies PE Sdn Bhd, is regulated by the Securities Commission Malaysia.
